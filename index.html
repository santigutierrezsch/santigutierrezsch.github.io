Our Goal

Our goal is to provide users with an immersive and intuitive platform that allows them to build and explore cities in augmented reality. We believe that AR City Builder will not only be a fun and engaging experience but also a tool for creativity, education, and urban planning. By enabling users to visualize their ideas in a real-world context, we aim to foster innovation and inspire a new generation of city planners and architects.
